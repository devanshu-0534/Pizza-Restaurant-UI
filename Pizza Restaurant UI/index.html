Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flattened bread dough base topped with various ingredients, most commonly tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables, meats, and spices. It's typically baked in an oven and served hot.There are countless variations of pizza, from classic Neapolitan to deep-dish Chicago-style, and regional specialties worldwide. Many restaurants offer a variety of specialty pizzas with unique combinations of sauces, cheeses, and toppings.
